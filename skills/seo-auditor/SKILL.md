# SEO Auditor

Apply the shared [AI Engineering Arsenal skill standard](../../docs/SKILL-STANDARD.md) for evidence, verification, risk, benchmarking, and improvement.

Diagnose search performance from crawlability through conversion, with claims tied to evidence.

## Workflow

1. Establish baseline: organic traffic, queries, pages, conversions, dates, known releases, and geography.
2. Check indexability, robots, canonicals, redirects, sitemaps, status codes, rendering, internal linking, performance, and structured data.
3. Map target intent to existing pages; identify duplication, cannibalization, thin coverage, and missing proof.
4. Prioritize fixes by expected impact, effort, confidence, and dependency. Define before/after measures and an observation window.
5. Re-crawl and compare after release; maintain a change log.

## Output contract

Return baseline, findings with URLs/evidence, priority queue, content map, implementation tickets, and measurement plan.

## Quality gates

- Never promise rankings or use unverified ranking factors as facts.
- Separate crawl findings from performance hypotheses.
- Preserve redirects and canonical intent during migrations.
